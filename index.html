Our Expert

Norman Foster is an expert architect and engineer known for his innovative and sustainable designs. He is the founder and chairman of Foster + Partners, an architecture firm with a global reputation for delivering high-quality, cutting-edge projects. Foster's early works include the Willis Faber and Dumas headquarters in Ipswich, and the Sainsbury Centre for Visual Arts in Norwich. He came to prominence with the completion of the Hongkong and Shanghai Bank in 1986. This building was notable for its energy-efficient design, which incorporated a number of environmentally friendly features such as natural ventilation, solar shading, and a rainwater harvesting system.
